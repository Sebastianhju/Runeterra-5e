**Time warp**

_Wizard, Sorcerer, Bard_

|level|Casting Time|Range/Area|Components|Duration|School|Attack/Save|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|0|1 Bonus Action|30 ft|V,S,M*|Instantainous|Transmutation|Wisdom|
